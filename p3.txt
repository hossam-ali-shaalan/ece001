	<h1>5 common myths about enterprise AI </h1>
	<h2>pages</h2>
<table>
 <tr>
   <td>The first myth</td>
   <td>Artificial intelligence requires your own style.
Fact: Most companies adopt artificial intelligence by combining internal and unconventional solutions. The development of the internal artificial intelligence allows companies to customize their unique business needs; Pre-prepared AI solutions enable you to simplify implementation with a ready-to-use solution to the most common business problems.</td>
   
 </tr>
 <tr>
   <td>The second myth</td>
   <td> Artificial intelligence provides instant magic results.
Truth: The path to AI success takes time, careful planning, and a clear idea of ​​the results you want to achieve. You need a strategic framework and iterative approach to avoid providing a random set of disconnected AI solutions.</td>
  
 </tr>
 <tr>
   <td>The third myth</td>
   <td> Artificial intelligence does not require people to operate it.
Truth: Artificial intelligence is not about controlling robots. The value of artificial intelligence is that it increases human capabilities and reduces the burden on your employees to allocate more strategic tasks. Moreover, artificial intelligence relies on people to provide it with the right data and work with it the right way.</td>
   
 </tr>
 <tr>
   <td>The fourth myth</td>
   <td>The more data, the better.
Fact: Enterprise AI needs smart data. In order to obtain the most effective business insights derived from artificial intelligence, your data must be of high quality, up-to-date, relevant and rich.</td>
  
 </tr>
<tr> 
<td>the fifth myth </td>
<td>AI Enterprise only needs data and models to succeed.
Truth: Data, algorithms, and models are just the beginning. But the AI ​​solution must be scalable to meet changing business needs. To date, most enterprise AI solutions have been designed by data scientists. These solutions require manual preparation, comprehensive formulas, and are not scalable. To successfully implement AI projects, you need scalable AI solutions to meet your needs as you advance into AI technology </td>
