	<h1>The factors behind the adoption of artificial intelligence
</h1>
<p>Provides a competitive advantage
Companies are increasingly realizing the competitive advantage of applying AI insights to business goals and making them a priority at the business level. For example, artificial intelligenceâ€™s targeted recommendations can help you make better decisions faster. And many of the features and capabilities of artificial intelligence can reduce costs, reduce risks, speed up market access time, and much more.
Large quantities of data are available for learning
Artificial intelligence needs to learn with lots of data to make the right predictions. The emergence of various tools for collecting disaggregated data, in addition to the ability of organizations to store and process this data easily and at an affordable cost, whether structural or non-structural data, has enabled more organizations to create and train artificial intelligence algorithms
Provides high-performance computing with ease and affordability. The abundance of computing power in business in the cloud, enabled easy access to computing with high performance and affordable prices. Prior to this development, the only computing environments available for AI were non-cloud-based and costly.
